Application Guide

Step-by-step instructions with tips

How to Apply STICKLS

1
Prep Your Nails

Push back cuticles, file nails short, and clean with the included prep pad for best adhesion.

2
Size & Test Fit

Find the perfect size for each nail. The press-on should cover your nail from sidewall to sidewall.

3
Apply Adhesive

Use adhesive tabs for temporary wear (3-5 days) or nail glue for longer wear (7-14 days).

4
Press & Hold

Place at a 45° angle and slowly lower. Press firmly for 15-30 seconds for secure attachment.

5
Shape & Finish

File to desired length and shape. Buff any rough edges for a professional finish.

Care Instructions

During Wear

  • Avoid prolonged water exposure for first 2 hours
  • Use gloves when cleaning or doing dishes
  • Apply cuticle oil daily to maintain nail health
  • File any chips immediately to prevent catching

Removal

  • Soak nails in warm soapy water for 10-15 minutes
  • Gently lift edges with a cuticle pusher
  • Never force or pull off the nails
  • Remove any residual adhesive with nail dehydrator

Storage for Reuse

  • Clean thoroughly with rubbing alcohol
  • Store in original packaging or nail box
  • Keep away from heat and direct sunlight
  • Label with size numbers for easy reapplication
